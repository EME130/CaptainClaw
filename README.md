## 🏴‍☠️ Wait — This Is Not That OpenClaw

You may have heard of *OpenClaw* recently. Hype. Stars. Agents. AI. Moltbot. Whatever they're calling it this week.

This is not that.

This is the *original* OpenClaw — **Captain Nathaniel Joseph Claw**, the greatest cat pirate to ever challenge the Cocker-Spaniard Armada. He was here first. He had god mode before it was a feature. He had nine lives before anyone was counting tokens.

---

## 🐱 The Story

<p align="center">
  <em>A 2D side-scrolling platformer by Monolith Productions, released September 5, 1997.</em>
</p>

A famous cat pirate — **Captain Nathaniel Joseph Claw**, also known as *The Surveyor of the Seven Seas* — is imprisoned by the Cocker-Spaniards after they attack and sink his ship. Waiting for execution in his cell at La Roca, he finds a note and a treasure map hidden in the wall, left by one Edward Tobin.

The note speaks of the **Amulet of Nine Lives** — a mystical artifact granting near-immortality.

He escapes. He fights. He *swashbuckles*.

## 🪦 A Moment of Silence — STOP THE WORLD

> *Let's do a full GC. Stop-the-world. And remember.*

[**Monolith Productions**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Monolith_Productions) — the studio that created Captain Claw — has been dissolved as of their Wikipedia page. The team that built this cult classic, hand-drew every sprite, recorded every snarky one-liner, and shipped a DVD edition *before DVDs were common* is no longer with us as a studio.

But we make them resurrect from the ashes.

They gave us *Blood*, *F.E.A.R.*, *Shadow of Mordor*, and somehow, squeezed between all of that, a 2D pirate cat platformer that became a cult classic. Stephan Weyte voiced both Caleb (Blood) and Captain Claw — in the same day, reportedly.

This project is a love letter to every programmer, artist, and sound designer who shipped that game in 1997.

## 🗺️ The Original Game

- **Released:** September 5, 1997 (North America)
- **Developer/Publisher:** Monolith Productions
- **Platform:** PC (Windows)
- **Genre:** 2D side-scrolling platformer
- **Cheat codes** (because they deserve to be remembered):
  - `MPGOD` — God mode
  - `MPSCULLY` — Skip level *(X-Files reference)*
  - `MPVADER` — Invulnerability *(Star Wars reference)*
  - `MPJORDAN` — Higher jump *(Michael Jordan reference)*
  - `MPGANDOLF` — Full Magic Claws *(LOTR reference, intentionally misspelled)*

The credits start normally, then get increasingly silly. That tracks.
